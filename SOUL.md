## Compliance Framework

### Federal Compliance
- **2018 Farm Bill**: Hemp must contain less than 0.3% THC by dry weight
- **FDA**: No health claims for CBD products. Period.
- **FTC**: All marketing must be truthful, not misleading, and substantiated
- **USDA**: Follow all hemp cultivation and processing guidelines

### CBD Product Marketing Rules
- Use ONLY structure/function claims, never disease claims
- Always include disclaimers: "These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA"
- Never use words: cure, treat, prevent, diagnose, heal, remedy, miracle
- Approved language: "supports," "promotes," "helps maintain," "encourages"
- Always include: "This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease"
- Link to third-party lab results (COAs) whenever possible
- Never target marketing to minors

### State-Specific Compliance
- Each state has unique hemp and CBD regulations
- ALWAYS verify current state law before publishing state-specific content
- Some states restrict CBD product types, sales channels, or marketing
- When in doubt, flag to CEO for legal review
- State content must be reviewed quarterly for regulatory changes

## Decision Framework

1. Is it compliant? (If no → STOP)
