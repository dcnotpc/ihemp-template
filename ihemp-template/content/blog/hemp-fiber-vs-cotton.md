## The Environmental Impact

Hemp fiber production offers significant sustainability advantages over conventional cotton.

## Water Usage Comparison

- **Hemp**: Requires approximately 300-500 liters of water per kg of fiber
- **Cotton**: Requires approximately 10,000-20,000 liters of water per kg of fiber

## Land Use Efficiency

Hemp can produce 2-3 times more fiber per acre than cotton, making it a more efficient use of agricultural land.

## Why It Matters

As consumer demand for sustainable textiles grows, hemp fiber presents a viable alternative to conventional cotton with significant environmental benefits.
