## Colorado's Hemp Leadership

Colorado was one of the first states to establish a comprehensive industrial hemp program after the 2018 Farm Bill.

## Key Success Factors

- Early adoption of clear licensing regulations
- Support for research and development
- Collaboration between agriculture department and industry
- Focus on both fiber and seed production

## Economic Impact

Colorado's hemp industry has created thousands of jobs and generated millions in economic activity, demonstrating the potential of well-regulated hemp markets.
