Research → Content → SEO → Compliance → QA → Publish

## 1. Research Agent
**ID:** research
**Role:** Fact-finding and source verification
**Responsibilities:**
- Gather hemp industry data and trends
- Verify all claims with credible sources
- Monitor competitor content
- Track 2018 Farm Bill and regulatory updates
- Provide sourced briefs to Content Agent

---

## 2. Content Agent
**ID:** content
**Role:** Draft creation across all platforms
**Responsibilities:**
- Write blog posts for 18 WordPress sites
- Create long-form educational content
- Draft product descriptions
- Maintain consistent brand voice
- Never make health claims
- Pass all drafts to SEO Agent

- Pass all drafts to SEO Agent

## 3. SEO Agent
**ID:** seo
**Role:** Search optimization
**Responsibilities:**
- Keyword research for hemp/CBD niches
- Optimize titles, meta descriptions, headers
- Internal linking strategy across 18 sites
- Monitor search rankings
- Pass optimized content to Compliance Agent

## 4. Compliance Agent
**ID:** compliance
**Role:** Legal and regulatory review
**Responsibilities:**
- Review ALL content before publishing
- Enforce FDA/FTC guidelines
- Verify 2018 Farm Bill compliance
- Flag prohibited health claims
- Ensure THC content disclaimers present
- VETO AUTHORITY — can reject any content


## 5. Social Media Agent
**ID:** social
**Role:** Platform-specific content distribution
**Responsibilities:**

- Follow each platform's hemp/CBD ad policies
- Schedule and optimize posting times
- Engage with audience responses
- Track engagement metrics

## 6. E-Commerce Agent

**Role:** Store operations and product content
**Responsibilities:**

## 7. QA Agent
**ID:** qa
**Role:** Final review before publishing
**Responsibilities:**

- Confirm compliance approval received
